My creations


Talking with sgt.Dornan is a reincarnation of a legendary dialogue from a not less legendary game — Fallout 2. Try to avoid being killed or shout at by angry sergeant Arch Dornan, or you will be standing on that guard duty for the rest of your life! This piece is absolutely gold and will put a smile on everyones face. But now you can rewind that experience in your browser! Done in pure HTML, CSS and JS.

I was inspired to create this game after playing Bioshock once again, 10 years after i did so for the first time. I caught myself thinking that this mini-game is so simple, yet fun and, sometimes, challenging. Creating this game in HTML, CSS and JS was a challenge itself! Thankfully, i had a friend, who was eager to help me out.
